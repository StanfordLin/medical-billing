Administration costs in healthcare are outrageous

In the U.S., more than 300 billion dollars are being spent on administrative costs alone. When you visit the doctor, you're also charged for the personnel needed to process billing and insurance-related activities like chasing after unpaid medical bills, negotiation of treatment costs, processing insurance claims and making sure the insurance on file is up-to-date.

Why we built Fair Price

It's faster. On average, it takes 5-6 months until a medical bill is processed through the insurance provider and given to the patient. With Fair Price, patients can walk out of the hospital with their medical bills in hand.

Fair prices. Calculated from a nationwide database of medical payment data from industry data and healthcare providers, we will help you find fair prices for medical treatments.

Reduce costs. By reducing the costly and labourious process of intepreting, negotiating, and determining costs, billions of dollars can be better utilised to deliver more effective treatments.
